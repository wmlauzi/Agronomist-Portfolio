About Me

Rooted in Science,
Grounded in Practice.

I am a crop systems agronomist with over 2 years of experience working at the intersection of soil health, climate adaptation, and food security. My work spans research institutions, NGOs, and government bodies across East and West Africa.

My approach combines traditional agronomic knowledge with precision agriculture technologies to create scalable, locally-appropriate solutions that improve livelihoods without compromising ecosystem health.

Research & Projects

Selected Work

2022 – Present · CGIAR
Regenerative Soil Carbon in Savanna Agroforestry Systems

A multi-country study measuring carbon sequestration potential of farmer-managed natural regeneration in semi-arid West Africa, working with 600 farms across Mali and Burkina Faso.

Agroforestry Carbon Sequestration Mali · Burkina Faso
2020 – 2023 · FAO
Drought-Tolerant Maize Varieties for East African Highlands

Field trials evaluating 22 DTMA varieties across altitude gradients in Kenya and Tanzania. Results informed national seed policy for three countries.

Crop Breeding Food Security Kenya · Tanzania
2019 – 2021 · World Bank
Precision Fertiliser Application via Mobile Soil Sensing

Piloted low-cost mobile soil testing devices with 200 extension officers to enable variable-rate fertiliser recommendations at plot level.

Precision Ag Soil Science Ethiopia
2017 – 2019 · USAID
Integrated Pest Management for Smallholder Vegetable Farmers

Developed and scaled a community-based IPM program for over 1,800 vegetable farmers in Northern Ghana, reducing pesticide costs by 40%.

IPM Horticulture Ghana
